About

Frank Ryan is DLA Piper's Global Chair and Global Co-CEO. Since the start of his leadership, Frank has driven unprecedented growth and innovation, guiding the firm in raising market share in key practices and sectors, developing world-class talent, and delivering impactful pro bono projects around the globe.

Frank is known as a go-to strategist for some of the world’s most sophisticated media and sports companies.

A graduate of Syracuse University College of Law, Frank now serves on the school's Board of Trustees. He is a member of the Legal Service Corporation's Leaders Council, advising the country's largest funder of civil legal aid programs in support of equal access to legal representation. Frank is the founding chair of DLA Piper's scholarship program, established to assist student athletes and veterans wishing to pursue a career in the legal profession. He is also a member of the Wall Street Journal CEO Council and Thomson Reuters' Global Strategy Board and is frequently quoted in business and legal media.
